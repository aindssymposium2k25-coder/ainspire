Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the registration process?

You can register through our online registration form. Click the "Register Now" button in the navigation or scroll down to find the registration section.

2. Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes! Each participant can join up to 3 events with combinations of 1 Technical + 2 Non-Technical or 2 Technical + 1 Non-Technical events.

3. Is there a registration fee?

Yes, the registration fee is â‚¹200 per participant. This covers access to all events, refreshments, and lunch.

4. Will certificates be provided?

All participants will receive a Certificate of Participation. Winners will receive additional certificates and prizes.

5. What should I bring to the event?

Bring your college ID card, and any personal items you might need throughout the day.

6. Is on-spot registration available?

Yes, on-spot registration will be available at the venue. However, we encourage participants to register online in advance to avoid last-minute rush.
