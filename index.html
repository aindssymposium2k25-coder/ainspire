Paper Presentation

Description

Present your innovative ideas and research papers on cutting-edge technologies in AI and Data Science. This event provides a platform to showcase your research work to a panel of experts.

Rules & Regulations

  • Maximum team size: 2 to 3 members
  • Duration: 5 to 7 minutes presentation + 3 minutes Q&A
  • PPT slides should be maximum 10 pages

Recognitions

  • 1st Place - Shield + Certificate of Excellance
  • 2nd Place - Shield + Certificate of Excellence

Data Analysis

Description

Showcase your analytical skills by deriving insights from complex datasets. This event allows you to demonstrate your ability to extract meaningful information from data. Participants will be given a dataset and specific questions to answer through their analysis.

Rules & Regulations

  • Team of 1 to 2 members
  • Duration: 30 minutes
  • Allowed tools: Python, R, Excel, Tableau
  • Internet access will be restricted
  • Pre-installed software only
  • Final submission should include code and report

Recognitions
  • 1st Place - Shield + Certificate of Excellance
  • 2nd Place - Shield + Certificate of Excellance

AI Art Battle

Description

Recreate the given image using AI tools within the time limit. The closer your artwork matches the original, the higher your chance to win!

Rules & Regulations

  • An original image will be shown to participants.
  • Participants must recreate the same image using AI tools.
  • Only approved AI tools (DALL·E, ChatGPT, Stable Diffusion, etc.) are allowed.
  • Time limit: 15-20 minutes.
  • Rewards will be given based on accuracy, similarity, and clarity of the recreated image

Recognitions
  • 1st Place - Shield + Certificate of Excellance
  • 2nd Place - Certificate of Excellance

Creative Animations

Description

Showcase your animation skills and creativity in this visual storytelling event. Create short animations on given themes using any animation software.

Rules & Regulations

  • Individual or team (max 3 members)
  • Allowed tools: Pencil2D or any other 2D animation software.
  • Content must be original and free from plagiarism.
  • Inappropriate, offensive, or copyrighted material is not allowed.
  • Judging will be based on creativity, originality, smoothness, and presentation.
  • Duration: 10-30 seconds

Recognitions

  • 1st Place - Shield + Certificate of Excellance
  • 2nd Place - Shield + Certificate of Excellance

Just A Minute

Description

Speak on a given topic for one minute without hesitation, repetition, or deviation. Quick thinking wins!

.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each participant will be given one paper with information.
  • One minute is given to read and memorize the content.
  • Participants must recall the content within the given time.
  • Marks are awarded based on accuracy, completeness, and clarity.
  • Any use of mobile phones or external help is strictly prohibited.

Recognitions

  • 1st Place - Shield + Certificate of Excellance
  • 2nd Place - Certificate of Excellance

Dumb Charades

Description

Act out movies, phrases, or words without speaking while your team guesses. Fun, fast, and full of laughter!

Rules & Regulations

  • Team size: 2 members (for/against)
  • Participants are not allowed to speak, write, or point to objects.
  • Only gestures and actions can be used to convey the clue.
  • Each team will be given a fixed time (e.g., 1 minute) to guess.
  • Correct guesses earn points; no points for skipped or wrong answers.
  • The team with the highest points at the end wins.

Recognitions

  • 1st Place - Shield + Certificate of Excellance
  • 2nd Place - Certificate of Excellance

Photography Contest

Description

Capture moments and showcase your photography skills. Submit your best shots based on the given theme.

Rules & Regulations

  • Individual participation only
  • Theme will be given on the spot.
  • Photos must be original and unedited (basic corrections allowed)
  • Format: JPEG

Recognitions

  • 1st Prize: Shield + Certificate of Excellence
  • 2nd Prize: Certificate of Excellence

BGM Finding

Description

Solve clues and find hidden treasures in this exciting adventure across the college campus. Test your problem-solving skills and teamwork.

Rules & Regulations

  • Team size: 1-3 members
  • Music clips will be played for a few seconds only.
  • Participants must identify the correct movie/series/song within the given time.
  • No use of mobile phones or external help is allowed.
  • Points are awarded for accuracy and speed.
  • In case of a tie, a bonus round will decide the winner.
